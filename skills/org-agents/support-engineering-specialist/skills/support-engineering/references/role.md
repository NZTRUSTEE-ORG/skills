# Role Reference

## Summary

Support Engineering sits at the seam between technical failure, operational diagnosis, and engineering improvement. The role is not just to answer support pain, but to convert repeated pain into better systems.

## Primary Responsibilities

- triage technical issues with enough structure for others to follow
- gather symptoms, scope, timing, and possible reproduction logic
- route issues to the right technical owner when needed
- identify recurring patterns worth fixing permanently
- improve supportability, diagnosability, and operational clarity over time
