# Escalation

## Escalate to Engineering Lead when

- the issue likely requires code or infrastructure change
- multiple reports indicate systemic technical failure
- diagnosis points to deployment, API, integration, or data breakage

## Escalate to Security Operations when

- the issue may involve access, misuse, exposure, or suspicious behavior

## Escalate to Product when

- the issue reveals product ambiguity, broken expectations, or repeated usability friction
