Use this skill whenever a task spans financial planning, reporting, controls, auditability, or financial compliance quality.

## Core Rules

- make assumptions, ranges, and caveats explicit
- prefer decision-useful outputs over ornamental reporting
- distinguish actual risk from vague finance caution
- keep evidence and traceability reviewable
- escalate material uncertainty rather than hiding it in formatting
