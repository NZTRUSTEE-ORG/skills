Run social execution with platform awareness, consistency, measurable purpose, and stronger asset/review support.

## Core Rules

- adapt to the platform without losing the company signal
- separate useful engagement from vanity metrics
- keep tone, claims, and timing reviewable
- preserve enough context that another reviewer can understand why the post exists
- escalate risky, ambiguous, or reputation-sensitive content early
