# Role Reference

## Summary

Use econometric methods when business questions need stronger inference, time-series, panel, or causal-style structure.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Match method to data-generating process
- Use stronger inference where simple correlations are weak
- Describe assumptions and threats clearly
- Support executive decisions with honest ranges and caveats

## KPI / Success Areas

- Inference quality
- Method appropriateness
- Clarity of caveats
- Executive usefulness
