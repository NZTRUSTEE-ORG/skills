# Role Reference

## Summary

Model binary and categorical outcomes such as conversion, churn risk, response probability, and adoption likelihood.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Choose logit/probit when the outcome warrants it
- Explain coefficients as decision-support, not mysticism
- Compare probability drivers across segments
- State limitations clearly

## KPI / Success Areas

- Probability model usefulness
- Segmentation clarity
- Interpretation quality
- Prediction support
