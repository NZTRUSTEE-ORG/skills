## Core Rules

- document decisions, exceptions, and evidence clearly
- prefer practical controls over vague compliance language
- distinguish serious risk from procedural untidiness
- keep outputs reviewable, auditable, and reusable
- escalate material ambiguity before it becomes silent drift
