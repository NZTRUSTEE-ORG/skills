# audit-review

Improve review quality and audit readiness through structured checks, evidence handling, and traceable conclusions.

## Core Rules

- make clear what was checked, what was not, and why
- preserve evidence in a form another reviewer can follow
- distinguish minor tidiness issues from material review concerns
- keep open items and exceptions explicit
- return review findings in a form that supports action, not just caution
