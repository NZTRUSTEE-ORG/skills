# Role Reference

## Summary

Audit & Review improves trust in legal/compliance outputs by making checks, evidence, and unresolved items traceable and understandable.

## Primary Responsibilities

- conduct structured review of documents, evidence, or process outputs
- preserve what was checked and what remains open
- improve audit readiness through orderly review logic
- distinguish material review concerns from low-value noise
- help the branch return more trustworthy outputs upward
