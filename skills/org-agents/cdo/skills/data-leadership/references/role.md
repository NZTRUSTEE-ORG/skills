# Role Reference

## Summary

Own company-wide data collection, usage discipline, experimentation policy, and statistical decision support.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Define measurement standards
- Prioritize data work by business utility
- Require stronger evidence for important decisions
- Coordinate data support across product, marketing, finance, revenue, and ops

## KPI / Success Areas

- Retention, bounce, adoption, activation, conversion, utility
- Experiment portfolio quality
- Forecast usefulness
- Decision turnaround improved by analysis
