## Core Rules

- tie campaigns and content to a real business objective
- keep attribution claims proportional to measurement quality
- avoid low-integrity growth tactics, misleading copy, or vanity-channel noise
- maintain consistency between positioning, product reality, and channel execution
- respect deliverability, reputation, and audience trust
