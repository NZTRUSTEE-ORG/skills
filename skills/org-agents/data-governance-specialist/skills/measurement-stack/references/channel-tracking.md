# Channel Tracking

## Wix + GTM

- Prefer a coherent GTM path rather than fragmented hardcoded snippets
- Avoid duplicate GA and GTM tracking on the same actions
- Verify pageview and event behavior after changes

## Email / Gmail / Workspace

- Distinguish click tracking from open tracking
- Use UTMs and destination tracking where appropriate
- Be careful with tracking pixels; note email-client limitations and privacy effects

## Ads / Attribution

- Keep campaign naming consistent
- Align ad click identifiers and analytics naming where possible
- Ensure conversions map cleanly back to business questions
