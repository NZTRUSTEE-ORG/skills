## Core Rules

- preserve evidence and workflow traceability as the work happens
- make exceptions and incomplete steps explicit
- prefer controls that are understandable and operable in practice
- separate material compliance risk from routine process untidiness
- return outputs in a form another reviewer can follow without re-discovery
