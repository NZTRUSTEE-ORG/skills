Turn product behavior signals into analysis that improves prioritization and interpretation.

## Core Rules

- analysis should improve a product decision or interpretation
- distinguish useful product signal from vanity or noise
- connect numbers to product context, not just dashboards
- preserve enough caveat and uncertainty for another reviewer to trust the output
- return findings in a form that helps prioritization, not just documentation
