Keep brand and creative outputs coherent, useful, and recognizable across channels.

## Core Rules

- keep marketing tied to a real business objective
- avoid overclaiming from weak attribution or weak product understanding
- maintain coherence between positioning, content, channels, and retention work
- escalate risky claims, misleading copy, or low-integrity tactics
