## Core Rules

- policies should have clear purpose, owner, and scope
- governance should clarify decision rights rather than blur them
- exceptions and interpretations should be explicit
- preserve enough traceability that another reviewer can follow the logic later
- avoid policy sprawl that creates paperwork without usable control
