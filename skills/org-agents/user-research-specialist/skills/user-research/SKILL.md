# user-research

Generate usable product insight from users so product decisions are less speculative and more grounded.

## Core Rules

- tie research to a decision or learning goal
- distinguish observations from interpretation
- avoid research theater disconnected from product choices
- preserve enough context that another reviewer can trust the synthesis
- return findings in a form that changes product understanding, not just fills a document
