# legal-compliance-leadership

Lead legal/compliance so trust documentation, controls, governance, and review quality protect the company without choking execution.

## Core Rules

- keep documentation and decisions reviewable
- separate material risk from procedural untidiness
- use practical, evidence-backed compliance language
- escalate ambiguity before it hardens into drift
