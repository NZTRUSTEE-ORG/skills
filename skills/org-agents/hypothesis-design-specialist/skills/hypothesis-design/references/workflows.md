# Workflows

## Standard Workflow

1. Clarify the decision and the business question.
2. Identify the metric, dataset, and instrumentation needed.
3. Check whether the task is descriptive, predictive, experimental, or inferential.
4. Choose the lightest method that answers the question credibly.
5. Produce a concise result with assumptions, limitations, and recommended next action.

## Escalate When

- instrumentation is weak or missing
- the question requires cross-functional implementation
- the claim being requested exceeds the evidence
