# Role Reference

## Summary

Email Lifecycle turns customer-state awareness into useful messaging sequences that support onboarding, activation, retention, re-engagement, and long-term value.

## Primary Responsibilities

- design or refine lifecycle sequences tied to customer state
- clarify segment, trigger, and message purpose
- monitor sender-quality and engagement health
- support retention and reactivation goals with reviewable messaging logic
- return lifecycle findings in a way the CMO branch can act on
