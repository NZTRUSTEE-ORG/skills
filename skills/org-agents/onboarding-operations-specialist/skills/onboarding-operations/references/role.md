# Role Reference

## Summary

Onboarding Operations improves the beginning of operational work by clarifying readiness, reducing avoidable confusion, and strengthening the first handoff into the wider system.

## Primary Responsibilities

- support onboarding flow and readiness
- identify missing information or unclear prerequisites early
- improve the quality of onboarding handoffs
- reduce recurring early-stage confusion
- help the wider operations system start work with fewer avoidable errors
